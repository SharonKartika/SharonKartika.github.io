dΠdQ=0 ⟹ dΠdQ=TRdQ−dCdQ=0 ⟹ MR-MC=0 ⟹ MR=MC \frac{d\Pi}{dQ} = 0\\ \implies \frac{d\Pi}{dQ}=\frac{\text{TR}}{dQ}-\frac{dC}{dQ}=0\\\implies \text{MR-MC}=0\\\implies \text{MR=MC} dQdΠ​=0⟹dQdΠ​=dQTR​−dQdC​=0⟹MR-MC=0⟹MR=MC

Thus, a firm will maximise its profits when marginal revenue is equal to the marginal cost.
